rules:
	
1 wasteland tile is in contact with 3 or more forest tiles				the wasteland tile becomes a grassland tile
1 wasteland tile in contact with    3 or more grassland tiles			the wasteland tile becomes a grassland tile
1 grassland tile is in contact with 3 or more wasteland tiles			the grassland tile becomes a wasteland tile
1 grassland tile is in contact with 3 or more forest tiles				the grassland tile becomes a forest tile
1 grassland tile is surrounded by   8 mountain or sand tiles			the grassland tile becomes a wasteland tile
1 grassland tile is in contact with 5 or more grassland tiles			the grassland tile becomes a wasteland tile
1 forest tile is in contact with    3 or more wasteland tiles			the forest tile becomes a grassland tile
1 forest tile is surrounded by      8 mountain or sand tiles			the forest tile becomes a grassland tile
1 forest tile is in contact with    5 or more forest tiles				the forest tile becomes a grassland tile
